Mental Health

Mental health refers to a person's emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It encompasses an individual's ability to manage stress, relate to others, make choices, and handle life's challenges. Good mental health is characterized by emotional resilience, a positive self-image, and the ability to cope with difficulties. Conversely, mental health issues can include conditions like depression, anxiety, bipolar disorder, and schizophrenia, which can affect thoughts, feelings, and behaviors. It's essential to prioritize and seek support for mental health, just as one would for physical health.
